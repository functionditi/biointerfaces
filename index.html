ABOUT

BIOINTERFACES is a project that exists within the intersection of man, nature and the machine. Speculative and set in a hybrid future, tangible and computational interactions can be facilitated through botanical matter as mediating interfaces. It aims to use notions of growth, decay and each humanâ€™s inherent biophilia to nurture direct, tactile relations with the environments around us.
